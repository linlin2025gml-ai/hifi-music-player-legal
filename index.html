3. Permission Usage / 三、权限使用说明

English

To provide its normal functions, this application may request the following permissions:

  • Audio/Media File Access: Used to scan, read, and play local music files stored on the user's device. This application does not upload, share, or collect the content of the user's audio files.
  • Notification Permission: Used to display music playback notifications and provide playback controls, such as play, pause, previous track, and next track.
  • Foreground Service and Wake Lock Permission: Used to maintain local music playback and playback controls while the application is running in the background or the device enters sleep mode.
  • Modify Audio Settings Permission: Used for music playback-related audio behavior, such as audio focus management, media volume control, playback audio attributes, high-fidelity playback, equalizer, and audio effects processing. This application will not use this permission to record audio, monitor the microphone, upload audio content, or modify phone calls, ringtones, or other system audio settings unrelated to this application's playback.

These permissions are used only for music playback-related functions and will not be used to upload, copy, sell, or share user files.

中文

为了实现正常功能，本应用可能申请以下权限：

  • 读取音频/媒体文件权限： 用于扫描、读取和播放用户设备中的本地音乐文件。本应用不会上传、共享或收集用户的音频文件内容。
  • 通知权限： 用于显示音乐播放通知以及提供播放、暂停、上一曲、下一曲等控制功能，方便用户在后台播放时进行操作。
  • 前台服务与唤醒锁权限： 用于在应用切换到后台或设备进入休眠状态时，保持本地音乐持续播放，并确保播放控制功能正常可用。
  • 修改音频设置权限： 用于与音乐播放相关的音频行为，例如音频焦点管理、媒体音量控制、播放音频属性、高音质播放、均衡器及音效处理。 本应用不会使用该权限进行录音、监听麦克风、上传音频内容，也不会修改通话、铃声或其他与本应用播放无关的系统音频设置。

上述权限仅用于实现音乐播放相关功能，不会用于上传、复制、出售或共享用户文件。
